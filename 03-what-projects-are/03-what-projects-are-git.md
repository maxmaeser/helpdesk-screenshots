Projects is your collaborative project management workspace. You can create Projects for you and your team, and you can have shared Projects with corporate that you collaborate on together. A typical Project covers a unit opening, a brand rollout, a campaign, anything with steps you and your team need to work through.

## Tasks and phases

A Project has two parts.

- **Tasks**: the work that needs to get done
- **Phases**: chapters that group tasks together

Tasks can live inside a phase, or sit flat at the Project level.

## Finding your way around

The **Projects** page lists your Projects in one view, with the name, type, owner, and a progress bar for each. Click a Project to open it and see the phases, tasks, and progress inside.

## Templates

A lot of Projects start from a template. Your brand can publish Project templates that launch with one click, so the structure, tasks, and assignments are already set up for you. Click **New project** to see the templates your brand has published.

## Task status

Each task carries a status, so you and anyone you've shared the Project with can see where things stand. One status worth knowing is **Blocked**: when a task is blocked, you'll see a short reason on the task, so whoever can clear it knows what's in the way.
