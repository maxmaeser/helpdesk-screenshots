Team is your roster of the people who operate the location with you. Each row shows a member's name, their role, the location they're assigned to, and their current status. Scan the whole roster from this page, and click any row to open the member's detail.

## Inviting a team member

1. Click **Add Team Member**.
2. Fill in their name, email, role, and location.
3. Click **Send invite**.

They get an email with a link to set up their account, and they show in your roster right away with a **Pending** status until they accept.

## The member detail

Click a member to see their role, the locations they're assigned to, their training progress, and when they were last active. This is where you manage one person's setup in one place.

## Roles

Roles are defined by your brand. The role dropdown shows the options your brand has set: owner, general manager, shift manager, staff, whatever your brand has standardized. Pick the one that matches what the person actually does at the location.

## How roles connect to work

The role does more than label the row. When your brand sets up a project template, each task has an Accountable role attached to it. When that project lands at your location, the task is assigned to whoever holds that role on your team. So if a task is Accountable to your general manager, your GM gets it. Change the role on a member and the project work follows.
