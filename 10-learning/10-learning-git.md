> **[VIDEO EMBED: Module 10 — Learning]**

Learning is where your brand's training lives. Three tabs sit at the top: **Courses**, **Learning Paths**, and **Certificates**.

## The Courses tab

The tab opens on **Pick up where you left off**, the courses you've started. Below it, a section called **Every course** holds the full catalog. Switch between grid and list view, and filter by category or status.

Each course card shows the cover, the title, a status badge, and the lesson count. Once you've started a course, a progress bar appears and the button reads **Continue Course**.

## Inside a course

Open a course and the course view loads. Chapters and their lessons sit in a sidebar on the left, with the current lesson highlighted. Video lessons play in the player, and a **Resources** panel below the player holds files you can download.

## Completing a course

Finish a lesson and it marks complete with a green check in the sidebar. Finish the last lesson and the course completes.

Learning is where you learn a new skill. When you need to look something up on the job instead, that's **Manuals**, covered in the next article.
