You can assign or reassign a lead to any agent whose assignability is set to **Automatic** or **Manual** for your brand. This is done directly from the leads table.

> Only agents whose assignability is set to **Automatic** or **Manual** appear in the assignment dropdown. If you do not see a team member listed, ask them to update their assignability on the **Sales Assignability** tab of the Profile page in their Account settings.

### Assigning a Lead to a Sales Rep

![Leads tab selected in Sales Pipeline, showing the leads table](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-assign-leads/leads-nav-tabs.png)

1. Navigate to **Sales → Pipeline** and select the **Leads** tab.
2. Find the lead you want to assign in the leads table.
3. Locate the **Assigned To** column for that lead.
4. Click the column cell to open the agent dropdown.
5. Select the sales rep you want to assign the lead to.

![Assigned To dropdown open in the leads table, showing the list of available sales reps](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-assign-leads/leads-assign-dropdown.png)
The assignment takes effect immediately. The selected agent receives a notification that a lead has been assigned to them.

### Reassigning a Lead

To reassign a lead to a different rep, follow the same steps above. Click the **Assigned To** column for the lead and select a different agent from the dropdown.

### Unassigned Leads

Leads that have not been assigned to any rep display **Unassigned** in the Assigned To column. This can happen when:

- A lead was imported without triggering auto-assignment
- No agents had their assignability set to **Automatic** when the lead was created
- The previously assigned agent has been removed from the team

### Bulk Assignment

To reassign multiple leads at once, select the leads you want to update using the checkboxes in the leads table, then use the bulk actions toolbar to assign them to a specific rep.

![Three leads selected with the Assign To Agent panel open showing available agents](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-assign-leads/assign-leads-bulk.png)
