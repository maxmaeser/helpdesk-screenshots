Notification preferences let you control how and when you receive alerts about activity in Franchise Systems Ai (FS Ai). Each notification type can be configured independently, so you only hear about what matters to you.

### Where to Find Notification Settings

1. Click your profile picture at the bottom of the sidebar and select **Settings**.
2. Go to the **Notifications** page.

![Profile menu with Settings option highlighted](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-set-up-your-notifications/notifications-profile-menu.png)
### How Notifications Work

Each notification type has two delivery channels you can toggle on or off:

- **Notify Me In-App**: Displays an alert inside the FS Ai dashboard. You can see these by clicking the notification bell icon.
- **Notify Me By Email**: Sends an email to the address associated with your account. Enabling email notifications also enables in-app notifications automatically.

If you turn off both options for a notification type, you will not receive that alert at all.

![Notifications settings page showing per-event toggles for in-app and email alerts](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-set-up-your-notifications/notifications-table.png)
### Available Notification Types

You can configure preferences for each of the following events:

- **New Sales Lead Assigned to Me**: A new applicant lead is assigned to you.
- **Multiple Sales Leads Assigned to Me**: Several leads are assigned to you at once.
- **Assigned Applicant Sends Chat Message**: An applicant you are working with sends a chat message.
- **Assigned Applicant Completes Form**: An applicant you are assigned to submits a form.
- **Assigned Applicant Books a Meeting**: An applicant schedules a meeting through your booking link.
- **Assigned a New Task**: You are assigned a new task.
- **Task Reminder**: A reminder fires for a task you are assigned to.
- **Task Status Changed**: A task you are involved with changes status.
- **Task Deleted**: A task you are assigned to is deleted.
- **Assigned Applicant Uploads a Document**: An applicant uploads a document.
- **Assigned Applicant Signs a Document**: An applicant completes a document signature.

### Mobile Push Notifications

If you use the FS Ai mobile app, push notifications are delivered to your device for supported events. Push notifications are sent automatically when you grant permission in the mobile app and do not require separate configuration in the dashboard.

### Tips

- Review your notification preferences after onboarding to ensure you are receiving the alerts most relevant to your role.
- If you are in a high-volume sales role, consider turning off in-app notifications for bulk assignment events and relying on email summaries instead.
