An organization is the top-level container in Franchise Systems Ai (FS Ai). It represents the parent entity - typically a franchise group or corporate office - that owns and manages one or more franchise brands.

### What an organization holds

Every organization includes:

- **Brands**: one or more franchise brands, each operating independently with its own data and configuration.
- **Team members**: agents (users) are invited to an organization and gain access to the brands within it.
- **Billing**: subscription management, payment methods, and invoicing are handled at the organization level.
- **Settings**: organization-wide preferences such as name, logo, timezone, and security policies (including two-factor authentication requirements).

### Access levels

When a team member joins an organization, they are assigned one of two access levels:

- **Admin**: can manage organization settings, invite and remove members, configure billing, and link or unlink brands.
- **Member**: can access the brands they have been granted permissions for, but cannot change organization-level settings.

An organization admin can also designate specific members as **billing admins**, giving them access to manage subscriptions and payment methods.

### How you join an organization

There are two ways to become part of an organization:

1. **Create one**: during onboarding, you set up a new organization as part of the getting started wizard.
2. **Join with an invite code**: an organization admin sends you an invite. You enter the code from the **Join Organization** option in the sidebar menu or from your account settings.

### Where to find it

You can view and manage your organization by clicking your profile picture at the bottom of the sidebar, selecting **Settings**, and opening the **Organization** page. This is where you see the organization name, linked brands, and current team members. Your associated organizations also appear on the **Profile** page under **Associated Organisations**.
