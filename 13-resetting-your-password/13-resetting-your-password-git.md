You can reset a forgotten password yourself from the Log In page, without waiting on your brand.

## Requesting a reset

On the **Log In** page, click **Forgot Password? Reset here.** That opens the **Reset your Password** page. Enter the email attached to your account and press **Reset Password**. The reset email comes from your brand's sender address, so if it doesn't land within a couple of minutes, check your spam folder before requesting another one.

## Setting a new password

Open the reset email and click the link inside. Choose a new password: a checklist on the page tracks the rules as you type, so you know it's strong enough. Press **Reset Password** to save it.

## Signing back in

Resetting your password returns you to the Log In page. It doesn't log you in automatically. Sign in there with your new password.

## If your link has expired

Reset links don't last forever. If yours has, you'll see an **Invalid Reset Link** message. Start again from **Forgot Password? Reset here.** on the Log In page.
