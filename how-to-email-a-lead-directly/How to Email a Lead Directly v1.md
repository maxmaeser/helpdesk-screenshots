You can send an email to an individual lead without setting up a newsletter or sequence. Open a lead's detail panel from **Sales → Pipeline**, click the **...** menu in the top right, and select **Send Email**.

![Lead detail panel with the three-dot menu open, showing the Send Email option](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-email-a-lead-directly/email-lead-menu.png)
This opens a modal with two options: use an existing email template or compose a new email from scratch.

![Send Email modal showing Use Template and Compose Email options](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-email-a-lead-directly/email-lead-choice.png)

### Using a Template

1. Select **Use Template**.
2. Browse or search your email templates in the list on the left. Only templates with email content appear here.
3. Click a template to see a live preview on the right with the lead's merge tags filled in (name, email).
4. Click **Next** to continue to send details.

![Template picker with a template selected and its preview on the right](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-email-a-lead-directly/email-lead-template.png)
If you have not created any email templates yet, the modal shows a link to **Sales → Workflows** on the **Content** tab where you can build one.

### Composing an Email

1. Select **Compose Email**.
2. Enter a subject line at the top.
3. Write your email in the editor on the left. You can format text with the toolbar and drag blocks from the panel on the right.
4. To personalize, insert merge tags for **first name**, **last name**, or **email** from the Insert section.
5. Click **Next** to continue to send details.

![Compose email editor with subject field, body editor, and content blocks panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-email-a-lead-directly/email-lead-compose.png)

### Send Details

After selecting or composing your email, you configure the send:

- **Purpose**: Choose the email purpose for this send. For sales leads, options include **Sales Newsletter (Consensual)** and **General Sales (Consensual)**. The purpose determines which verified domain is used as the sender.
- **Send Immediately**: Enabled by default. Toggle it off to schedule the email for a specific date and time instead.

![Send details showing the Purpose dropdown open and scheduling options](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-email-a-lead-directly/email-lead-send-details-v2.png)
Click **Send** to deliver the email. You can confirm delivery in the lead's **Activity** tab, where the sent email appears as an activity entry. Click the email name to view the full message that was sent.

### Other Contact Types

This feature is also available for marketing prospects and franchisees. Open a prospect from **Marketing → Audiences** or a franchisee from the franchisee panel, click the **...** menu, and select **Send Email**. The workflow is the same, but purpose options vary by contact type.
