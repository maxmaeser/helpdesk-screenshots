If your organization manages more than one franchise brand, Franchise Systems Ai (FS Ai) lets you switch between brands or view data across multiple brands depending on which area of the dashboard you are in.

### How Brand Selection Works

A brand selector appears at the top of most dashboard pages. How it behaves depends on the section you are viewing:

- **Single-brand sections** (Sales pipeline, Operations, Portal Editor, Brand Settings, and others): You work with one brand at a time. Click a brand in the selector bar to switch to it. The page content updates to show data for the selected brand only.

![Single Brand Selector](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/multi-brand-view/single-brand-selector.png)

- **Multi-brand sections** (Home, Sales list, Marketing list): You can select multiple brands at once to see aggregated data. Click individual brands to toggle them on or off, or use the **View All** button to select every brand you have access to.

![Multi-brand selector with all brands selected](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/multi-brand-view/multi-brand-selector.png)

### Switching Brands

1. Look at the brand selector bar at the top of the page.
2. Your currently active brand is highlighted.
3. Click a different brand to switch to it.
4. If more brands are available than can fit in the bar, click **Other Brands** to open a dropdown with a searchable list of all your brands.

Your brand selection is saved per section. When you return to a page, it remembers which brand you last selected there.

### Reordering Your Brands

If you work with many brands, you can customize which ones appear in the quick-access bar at the top:

1. Click **Other Brands** to open the brand dropdown.
2. Click **Edit Order** in the bottom right of the dropdown.
3. Drag and drop brands between the top bar (up to 5 brands) and the dropdown list.
4. Click **Save** to apply your new order, or **Cancel** to discard changes.

![Other Brands dropdown with Edit Order](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/multi-brand-view/other-brands-dropdown.png)
### Brand Access and Permissions

You can only see brands that your organization has access to and that your account has been granted permissions for. If you expect to see a brand but it does not appear in the selector, ask your organization admin to verify your brand-level permissions.
