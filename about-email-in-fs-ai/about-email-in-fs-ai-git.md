Franchise Systems Ai (FS Ai) handles email across different contexts: cold outreach, sales, marketing, franchisee communications, and system notifications. Understanding cold vs warm email helps you set up your domains correctly.

### Cold Email vs Warm Email

**Cold email** goes to recipients who have not opted in. It carries higher spam risk and can damage your sender reputation. FS Ai maps this to the **Cold** domain purpose so cold outreach stays isolated from your other email.

**Warm email** goes to recipients who have opted in or have an existing relationship with your brand: sales emails, newsletters, consensual marketing, and franchisee communications.

Mixing cold and warm on the same domain risks deliverability problems for both.

### Why FS Ai Supports Multiple Email Domains

Different email types carry different reputation risk. Assigning each domain a specific purpose isolates that risk. Your transactional and portal notification emails stay protected even if cold outreach encounters deliverability issues.

### Sending Email in FS Ai

FS Ai provides two ways to send email:

1. **Bulk sends**: Deploy a single email template as a newsletter, or send a multi-email sequence to a segment. You choose the segment, schedule, and which domains to use.
2. **Automations**: Trigger-based workflows that send to individual contacts in response to events (new lead created, tag applied). These fire one at a time and are not bulk sends.
