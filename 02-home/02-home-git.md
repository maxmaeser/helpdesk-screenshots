Home is the snapshot of your business. It surfaces the need to know information and recent updates, so the things you'd otherwise hunt for are already waiting when you log in.

## What you'll find

- **Messages**: your latest threads, with the newest at the top
- **Tasks**: your active and upcoming tasks, with due dates and status
- **Quick links**: shortcuts into the rest of the portal

Panels for manuals, the brand feed, assets, and your team round out the page.

## How to use it

Start your day here. Hover a task to see its due date and status, open a message to jump into the thread, and use the quick links to move into the section you need. Home is the first page you see when you log in, so the current picture of your business is waiting for you.
