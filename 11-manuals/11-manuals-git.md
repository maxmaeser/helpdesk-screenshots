> **[VIDEO EMBED: Module 11 — Manuals]**

Manuals is your operating reference: the brand's playbooks, SOPs, and standards. Learning is where you learn a new skill through sequenced courses. Manuals is where you look something up on the job. Two surfaces, two different jobs.

## Categories

Categories live on the left rail: operations, food safety, HR, marketing, real estate, accounting, whatever structure your brand uses to organize the reference. Pick a category and you see the manuals that belong to it.

## Reading a manual

Each manual opens in a clean document view. A table of contents sits on the left so you can jump to a section, and the body is laid out for reading on the floor or at a desk.

## Search

Search runs across the whole library. Type a phrase, and the results show the manuals that match with the matched text highlighted, so you can land on the exact paragraph that answers your question.

## Good to know

Manuals is read only for franchisees. The source of truth is maintained by your brand, and when corporate updates a manual, the new version is what you see. Courses you complete in Learning often point back here, so think of Manuals as the long form reference behind the training.
