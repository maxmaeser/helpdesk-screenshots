Task cards work the same way across your portal. The chip in the top corner tells you the type of task. The button at the bottom tells you what to do. Read the chip, click the button, and the portal walks you through it.

## The ten task types

- **To Do**: a manual task you confirm in the app. Do the work in the real world, then click **Mark Complete** to log it.
- **File Upload**: asks for a document or file. Drop the file into the card and it's attached to the task.
- **Form**: a structured set of questions. Fill in the fields and click **Submit**, and your answers are saved to the task record.
- **Link**: sends you to an external URL. Click **Open Link**, review the page, then mark the task complete.
- **Slides**: a deck to review. Page through the slides and mark the task complete when you reach the end.
- **Video**: plays a video in the task. Watch through to the end, then mark the task complete.
- **eSignature**: opens a document for signing through the integrated signing tool. Sign it, and the signed copy lands back in the task.
- **Meeting**: books a meeting on a calendar. Pick a time that works, confirm the booking, then mark the task complete.
- **Quiz**: a set of questions with a pass or fail result. Pass it, and the portal records your certification on the task.
- **Survey**: collects your feedback. Answer the questions and submit, and your response is recorded.

## Tasks with approvers

Some tasks have approvers attached. When you finish a task with a required approval, it moves into a pending state until the approver signs off. The next article, Working with Approvers, covers how that works.
