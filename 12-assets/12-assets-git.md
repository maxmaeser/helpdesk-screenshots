Assets is your brand's file library. It's where the photos, logos, video clips, marketing templates, and forms your brand has shared sit in one place, ready for you to download and use.

## The five categories

- **Photos**: the approved image library. Product shots, lifestyle photography, location imagery, anything your brand has cleared for you to use locally.
- **Logos**: the brand mark library. Primary and secondary marks, light and dark variants, and file formats sized for print, web, and signage.
- **Video Clips**: short form footage for social posts and local marketing. Each clip is tagged so you can find what fits the moment.
- **Marketing Templates**: editable creative. Social graphics, flyers, on site signage, the layouts your brand has built for you to drop your unit's details into.
- **Forms**: the document side of the library. Onboarding paperwork, vendor forms, customer facing handouts, anything your brand has standardized for download.

## Downloading a file

The flow is the same across categories. Open a file, review the usage notes, and click **Download**. Files come down at the resolution and format your brand has approved.

## Brand and Assets

Brand is the standards layer: the visual rules, the voice, the customer experience your brand defines. Assets is the file side: the actual photos, logos, and templates that bring those standards to life. You'll see them cross reference each other often.
