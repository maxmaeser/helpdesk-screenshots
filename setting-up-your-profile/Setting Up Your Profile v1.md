Your profile contains your personal details, contact information, and lead assignment preferences. You can update it at any time from the Settings page.

### Accessing Your Profile

1. Click your name or avatar at the bottom of the sidebar.
2. Select **Settings**.
3. Under **Profile**, click **Profile Details**.

![Settings sidebar with Profile Details selected, showing the Details page with avatar upload and form fields](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/setting-up-your-profile/profile-nav-settings.png)

### Editing Your Details

On the **Profile Details** page you can update the following fields:

- **First Name** and **Last Name**
- **Email** (used for login and notifications)
- **Phone**
- **LinkedIn URL**
- **Primary Job Title**

Make your changes and click **Save** to apply them.

![Profile details form showing First Name, Last Name, Email, Phone, LinkedIn URL, and Job Title fields](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/setting-up-your-profile/profile-details.png)

### Setting Your Lead Assignability

Under **Agent Sales Assignability**, you control how leads from the applicant portal are assigned to you for each brand.

Each brand has three options:

- **Automatic** -- You are included in the automatic lead assignment rotation.
- **Manual** -- You can only be assigned leads manually by another team member.
- **Not Assignable** -- You will not receive any leads for this brand.

![Assignable To Applicant Portal Leads section showing three brands with Automatic, Manual, and Not Assignable radio buttons](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/setting-up-your-profile/profile-assignability.png)

> If a team member does not appear in the assignment dropdown when assigning a lead, they need to update their assignability to **Automatic** or **Manual** on this page.
