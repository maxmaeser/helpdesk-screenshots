Vendors is the brand approved partner list you order from. It covers the supplier and partner network your brand has cleared for you to work with: food, packaging, tech, services, anything operational that comes from outside the four walls of your unit. The brand vets these partners and keeps the list current.

## The vendor grid

The main view is a grid of cards. Each card shows the vendor name, the category they fall under, a primary contact, and an ordering link. Click into a card and the detail view opens with the full vendor record, contact info, the ordering link, and any notes the brand has added about how to work with this partner.

## Three actions on a vendor

- **Place Order**: opens the vendor's ordering link in a new tab. Some brands wire this directly to a vendor portal, others link to a price list or order form. Either way, this is the path to a new order.
- **Contact Vendor**: pulls up the vendor's primary contact info. Email, phone, and any account notes the brand has set for this partner. Use this for questions about an order or account setup.
- **Report Issue**: for problems that need corporate to know. Late delivery, quality issues, billing disputes. The form routes straight to corporate and the brand handles it from there.

## How the list works

Vendors here are brand controlled. The brand decides who's on the list, who comes off, and what categories the network covers. Ordering happens through approved partners. If you have a vendor in mind that isn't on the list, that's a conversation with your franchise team.
