This is the stage where an empty account becomes a working one.

You have an organization and your first brand.

Now you bring in the people, billing, and brand details that make the platform usable day to day.

Most of this work happens in your settings, which you reach by clicking your profile picture at the bottom of the sidebar.

You are in this stage before any leads exist, so there is no rush.

Get the foundation right and everything that follows has somewhere to land.

### What to set up

Work through these in roughly this order. Each one links to a full how-to.

1. *[How to Invite Users]*: add your teammates to the organization by email so they can log in and start working.
2. *[How to Set Up Your Notifications]*: choose which activity you get alerted about so you only hear about what matters.
3. *[How to Update Your Payment Method]*: confirm the card on file so your subscription stays active.
4. *[How to View Your Invoices]*: find your organization's billing history whenever you need it.
5. *[Multi-Brand View]*: understand how brand switching works if you manage more than one brand.
6. *[How to Edit Your Portal]*: shape the applicant-facing pages and steps your leads see after signing up.
7. *[How to Build a Form]*: collect the information you need from applicants along the way.
8. *[How to Connect Your Calendar]*: sync Google or Office 365 so events and scheduling live on your Home page.

> **Permissions are set per person.** Access in Franchise Systems Ai (FS Ai) is granted to one individual at a time, never to a whole department, region, or group. See *[Understanding Roles and Permissions]*.

Once your team is in and your brand is configured, you are ready to put people into the system.

**Next up:** *[Get Your Leads In]*, where you import and organize the prospects and leads that fill your pipeline.
