Franchise Systems Ai (FS Ai) syncs with your Google Calendar or Office 365 calendar so you can view, create, and manage events without leaving the dashboard. Once connected, your external calendar events appear directly on your Home page.

> **Prerequisite**: You need an active Google or Microsoft Office 365 account with calendar access.

### Supported Calendar Providers

- **Google Calendar**
- **Office 365 Calendar**

You can connect one calendar account at a time. To switch providers, disconnect your current calendar first, then connect the new one.

### Connecting Your Calendar

1. Click your profile picture at the bottom of the sidebar and select **Settings**.
2. Under the **Account** group, open **Calendar & Scheduling**.
3. On the **Calendars** tab, click **Connect Google Calendar** or **Connect Office 365 Calendar**.

![Connect a calendar dialog on the Calendars tab, listing Google Calendar and Office 365 as provider options](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-connect-your-calendar/calendar-connect-provider-modal.png)
4. A popup window opens asking you to sign in and authorize access.
5. Complete the authorization in the popup. The calendar connects automatically once approved.

Booking events that prospects use to schedule meetings with you live on the **Event types** tab of the same page.

![Event types tab on the Calendar & Scheduling page, listing the booking events available under this calendar](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-connect-your-calendar/calendar-settings-event-types-tab.png)
### Calendar Views

Once connected, the Calendar tab on your Home page displays your events in three views:

- **Month**: a full-month grid showing event titles on each day
- **Week**: a detailed hourly breakdown of your current week (this is the default view)
- **Day**: an hourly breakdown of a single day

Switch between views using the dropdown in the calendar toolbar. Use the left and right arrows next to the date label to navigate forward or backward in time.

### Timezone Support

The calendar toolbar includes a **timezone selector** on the right side. Changing the timezone adjusts how event times are displayed. Your browser's local timezone is selected by default.

### Creating Events

1. Navigate to **Home** and select the **Calendar** tab.
2. Click the **Create Event** button in the toolbar (top right). A panel slides open on the right side of the calendar.
3. Fill in the event details:
   - **Event Title** (required)
   - **Date and Time**: pick a date from the date picker and set start/end times
   - **Description**: add notes using the rich text editor
   - **Location**: enter a physical address or location name
   - **Meeting Link**: toggle this on to generate an online meeting link (Google Meet or Microsoft Teams, depending on your provider)
   - **Guests**: type an email address and click the add button to invite attendees
   - **Guest Permissions**: control whether guests can edit the event, invite others, or see the guest list
4. Click **Create New Event** to save.

The event syncs to your connected calendar and attendees receive an invitation.

### Viewing and Managing Events

Click any event on the calendar to open a detail popover showing the event title, date and time, organizer, description, location, meeting link (if applicable), and guest list with their RSVP status.

From the event detail view you can:

- **Respond to invitations**: click **Yes**, **No**, or **Maybe** at the bottom of the event detail to set your attendance
- **Edit an event**: click the pencil icon to modify event details (available if you are the organizer or have edit permissions)
- **Delete an event**: click **Delete Event** while editing (available to organizers only; attendees are notified of the cancellation)
- **Join a meeting**: click the **Join Meeting** button next to the date to open the Google Meet or Microsoft Teams link

### The Calendar Sidebar

On the Home page **Overview** tab, a sidebar shows your upcoming events for the next two weeks. This gives you a quick agenda-style snapshot without switching to the full Calendar tab. You can click any event in the sidebar to view its details, or click **Create New Event** at the top to add one.

### Disconnecting Your Calendar

1. Click your profile picture at the bottom of the sidebar and select **Settings**.
2. Under the **Account** group, open **Calendar & Scheduling** and stay on the **Calendars** tab. Your connected account email and provider are displayed.

![Calendars tab showing a connected Google Calendar account with its email, Primary and Connected badges, and the Manage control](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/how-to-connect-your-calendar/calendar-settings-connected.png)
3. Click to manage the connection and choose **Disconnect Calendar**.
4. Confirm the disconnection in the dialog that appears.

> **Note**: Disconnecting your calendar means leads can no longer book calls with you through scheduling links.

### Reconnecting After Authorization Expires

If your calendar provider's authorization expires or becomes invalid, the dashboard shows a prompt asking you to reauthorize. Reconnect from the **Calendars** tab under **Calendar & Scheduling** in your settings. Until you reauthorize, leads cannot schedule meetings with you.
