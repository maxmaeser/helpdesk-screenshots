> **[VIDEO EMBED: Creating Your Account & Logging In]**

Your franchisee account starts with an invite from your brand. Once you accept it, you're a few steps away from your first login.

## Accepting your invite

Your brand sends an invite email with the subject "Your Invite to the {Brand} Franchisee Dashboard." Open it and click **Accept Invitation**. That takes you to the account creation page with your **Invite Code** and **Email** already filled in and locked. Add your **First Name**, **Last Name**, and **Phone**, then press **Continue**.

![Account creation form with locked Invite Code and Email fields, plus name and phone fields](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/00-creating-your-account/account-signup-form.png)
## Choosing a password

Next you set a password. It needs to be 10 characters or more, with at least one lowercase letter, one uppercase letter, one number, and one special character. A checklist on the page tracks each rule and turns it green as you meet it, so you know exactly what's left before you press **Create Account**.

## Verifying your email

A **Check Your Email** screen appears next. The portal sends a verification link to your inbox. Quick-open buttons for Gmail, Outlook, Yahoo, and iCloud Mail take you straight there, or use **Resend Email** if it hasn't arrived yet. Click the link in the email and you land straight on your portal Home, already logged in.

## Signing back in

From then on, sign in at the portal's **Log In** page with your email and password. If the invite link ever tells you the invite code is no longer valid, ask your brand contact to send you a fresh invite.
