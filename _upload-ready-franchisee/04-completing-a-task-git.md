Task panels work the same way across your portal. Each one shows the type of task and its due date, plus an action button that matches what the task needs. Click the button, and the portal walks you through it. Once you finish, the task gets a **Task complete** confirmation with a large green check, and back in the project's task list it shows a green **Done** badge with the name crossed out.

## The seven task types

- **Todo checklist**: a manual task you confirm in the portal. Do the work in the real world, then click **Mark Complete** to log it.
- **Form**: a structured set of questions. Fill in the fields and click **Submit**, and your answers are saved to the task record.
- **Visit link**: sends you to an external URL. Click **Open Link**, review the page, then mark the task complete.
- **Schedule a call**: books a meeting on a calendar. Pick a time that works, confirm the booking, then mark the task complete.
- **Watch video**: plays a video in the task. Watch through to the end, then mark the task complete.
- **View slides**: a deck to review. Page through the slides and mark the task complete when you reach the end.
- **Signature**: opens a document for signing through the integrated signing tool. Sign it, and the signed copy lands back in the task.

## Attachments

Tasks have an attachments area where you can add files to support your work. Upload a file straight from your device, or attach one you've already saved in Assets.

## Tasks with approvers

Some tasks have approvers attached. When you finish a task with a required approval, it moves into a pending state until the approver signs off. The next article, Working with Approvers, covers how that works.
