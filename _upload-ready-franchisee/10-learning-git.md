> **[VIDEO EMBED: Module 10 — Learning]**

Learning is where your brand's training lives. Three tabs sit at the top: **Courses**, **Learning Paths**, and **Certificates**.

## The Courses tab

The tab opens on **Pick up where you left off**, the courses you've started. Below it, a section called **Every course** holds the full catalog. Switch between **Grid** and **Cards** view, and filter by category or status.

![Courses tab showing the catalog of available courses](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/10-learning/learning-courses.png)
Each course card shows the cover, the title, a status badge, and the lesson count. Once you've started a course, a progress bar appears. In **Cards** view the card also gets a button, reading **Continue Course** or **Start Course** before you've begun. **Grid** view doesn't show that button: the whole card is clickable, so tap it to open the course.

![A started course card showing the Continue Course button](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/10-learning/learning-course-card.png)
## Inside a course

Open a course and the course view loads. Chapters and their lessons sit in a sidebar on the left, with the current lesson highlighted. Video lessons play in the player, and a **Resources** panel below the player holds files you can download.

![Course view showing chapters, the video player, and the Resources panel](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/10-learning/learning-course-view.png)
## Completing a course

Finish a lesson and it marks complete with a green check in the sidebar. Finish the last lesson and the course completes.

Learning is where you learn a new skill. When you need to look something up on the job instead, that's **Manuals**, covered in the next article.
