Projects is your collaborative project management workspace. You can create projects for you and your team, and you can have shared projects with corporate that you collaborate on together. A typical project covers a unit opening, a brand rollout, a campaign, anything with steps you and your team need to work through.

## Tasks

A project is made up of tasks: the steps you and your team need to complete. Open a project and its tasks are grouped into buckets by status, so you can see at a glance what's still to do, what's in progress, what's waiting on an approver in **Awaiting approval**, or what's done.

## Finding your way around

The **Projects** page lists your projects in one view, with the name, status, next action, progress, and due date for each. Click a project to open it and see its tasks and progress inside.

## The project overview

Open a project and click **Overview** in the top corner to see a summary of the project: its start date, current status, and the locations it's linked to. It's a quick way to check scope without digging through every task.

![Project Overview popover showing summary, start date, and linked locations](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/03-what-projects-are/projects-overview.png)
## Task status

Each task carries a status, so you and anyone you're sharing the project with can see where things stand: **To-do**, **In progress**, or **Done**.

Some tasks depend on others finishing first. If a task's prerequisites aren't done yet, it shows as **Locked** with a lock icon instead. Once the tasks ahead of it wrap up, it unlocks and you can get started.

![A Locked task row with a lock icon indicating a dependency gate](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/03-what-projects-are/projects-locked.png)
