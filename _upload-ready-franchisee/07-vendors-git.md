> **[VIDEO EMBED: Module 7 — Vendors]**

Vendors is the brand approved partner list you order from. It covers the supplier and partner network your brand has cleared for you to work with: food, packaging, tech, services, anything operational that comes from outside the four walls of your unit. The brand vets these partners and keeps the list current.

## The vendor list

The main view is a table. Each row shows the vendor name, a usage badge (**Required**, **Recommended**, or **Optional**), the category the vendor falls under, how many of your locations it's assigned to, and contact info: email and phone. Click a row and the detail view opens with five tabs: **Details**, **Contacts**, **Locations**, **Manuals**, and **Assets**. Details holds the vendor record and any notes your brand has added about working with this partner. Contacts holds the people to reach for order or account questions. Locations, Manuals, and Assets hold setup info, documentation, and files specific to that vendor.

## Setup status

The Locations tab tracks where this vendor stands for each of your units with a **Setup Status**: **Not Implemented**, **Implementing**, or **Implemented**. Update it as you work through getting a vendor in place, so your brand can see where things stand without having to ask.

![Vendor Setup Status menu showing Not Implemented, Implementing, and Implemented](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/07-vendors/vendors-setup-status.png)
## How the list works

Vendors here are brand controlled. The brand decides who's on the list, who comes off, and what categories the network covers. Ordering happens through approved partners. If you have a vendor in mind that isn't on the list, that's a conversation with your franchise team.
