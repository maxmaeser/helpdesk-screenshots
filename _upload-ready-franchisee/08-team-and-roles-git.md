> **[VIDEO EMBED: Module 8 — Team and roles]**

Team is your roster of the people who operate the location with you. Each row shows a member's name, email, and current status, along with the controls you use to set their role. Scan the whole roster from this page, and click any row to open the member's detail.

![Team roster showing each member's name, email, and status](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/08-team-and-roles/team-roster.png)
## Inviting a team member

1. Click **Invite teammate**.

![Invite teammate modal with fields for name, email, title, and reason](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/08-team-and-roles/team-add-member.png)
2. The modal walks you through a short set of steps. Start with their full name, email, and job title, then continue through the rest, including why you're inviting them.
3. Submit it.

They get an email with a link to set up their account, and they show in your roster right away with a **Pending** status until they accept.

## The member detail

Click a member to open their detail panel. It shows their **Primary** or **Consulted** badge, along with their email, phone, and the date they joined. If someone needs to come off your team, you can remove them from here too.

![Member detail panel showing Primary or Consulted badge, email, phone, and joined date](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/08-team-and-roles/team-member-detail.png)
## Roles

Roles are defined by your brand, and you set them from the team list itself: each active member gets a **Primary** radio and a **Consulted** toggle right on their row. Primary marks the one person accountable, so only one teammate holds it at a time. Consulted marks anyone who should stay in the loop without owning it. Switching someone to Primary opens a confirmation, since it changes who's on the hook.

![Role controls on a roster row showing the Primary radio and Consulted toggle](https://raw.githubusercontent.com/maxmaeser/helpdesk-screenshots/master/08-team-and-roles/team-role-controls.png)
## How roles connect to work

The role does more than label the row. When your brand sets up a project template, each task has an Accountable role attached to it. When that project lands at your location, the task is assigned to whoever holds that role on your team. So if a task is Accountable to your general manager, your GM gets it.
